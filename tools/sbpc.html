super border (bit)packing converter

converts 8bpp bitmaps to 15 color bitpacked super game boy borders
drop bitmaps here
note: the tool is currently not fully functional
how to use:
  1. draw a border (256x224 256 color bitmap, but dont use more than 15 colors.
    the middle 160x144 for the game screen is automatically removed)
  2. upload file here to convert to 4bpp, pct and pal
  3. do whatever you want with the output

how is this better than superfamiconv?

its only better if you have a lot of tiles that use 3 or less colors, or a mix of 5 and 3.
additionally, colors are picked after tile deduplication, so if you have a lot of empty tiles, they will not interfere with the bitpacking of tiles that are more fit for that
tile flips are currently unsupported
